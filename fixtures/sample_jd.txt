Job Title: Business Intelligence Analyst

We are seeking a Business Intelligence Analyst to join our growing analytics team. You will own dashboard development, data pipeline reporting, and cross-functional stakeholder communication.

Responsibilities:
- Build and maintain BI dashboards (Power BI or Tableau) for executive and operational reporting
- Write complex SQL queries against our data warehouse to support ad hoc and recurring reporting needs
- Use Python for data automation and ETL scripting
- Partner with marketing, sales, and operations stakeholders to define KPIs and reporting requirements
- Identify data quality issues and work with engineering to resolve them
- Present findings and recommendations to senior leadership

Requirements:
- Bachelor's Degree in a quantitative field (Statistics, Economics, Computer Science, or related)
- 3+ years of experience in business intelligence, data analysis, or reporting
- Strong SQL skills required
- Experience with Python for data manipulation (pandas)
- Experience with Tableau or Power BI
- Excellent stakeholder communication and presentation skills
- Strong attention to detail and data quality mindset
